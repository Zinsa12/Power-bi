
"WattWatch: Analyzing Urban Energy Consumption for Smarter Cities"
Central zone recorded the highest average energy consumption, indicating it is the primary energy hotspot.
Industrial consumers account for the largest share of electricity usage compared to residential and commercial consumers.
Energy consumption peaks during September, suggesting seasonal demand fluctuations.
North and South zones experienced higher outage durations, indicating potential power supply reliability issues.
Average peak usage remains consistently high among industrial consumers, increasing load on the grid.
Residential consumption is lower overall but shows steady demand throughout the year.
Targeted energy-efficiency programs in high-consumption zones could reduce overall city power demand and operational costs.
